Content

Home page

This is where I will lightly introduce my website. I don't need it to be anything busy, just a easy welcome page that allow users to feel comfortable moving from page to page. By having a non-busy home page, it'll give me more opportunity on other pages to expand on who I am and what skills I possess. This will also attract more users since people don't like entering clutterly websites.

Images for the Home page

About

This part is where I would introduce myself and who I am. I want to explain roughly where I'm from and what I like to do. I'll then go on and explain my skills and experience that I have from this career. I'll also talk about what other skills I could bring to the table as an employee and reference my projects page to guide them there to see what I'm capable of.

Images for the About Page

Projects

This page is where I will show off some of my projects that I completed whether by myself or in classes from bootcamp or college. I will also explain 2-3 projects and what they are supposed to accomplish in the real world. With that, I'll explain what parts I played in while putting those projects together. By doing this it will allow me to help the visitors examine those areas and have them see what I can do.
